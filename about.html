Eva & The Big Deals

About

Eva and The Big Deals is a rock group from Louisville, KY. Formed in May of 2019 by lead singer Eva the Diva, the band consists of 5 members from various musical backgrounds. Joining Eva is The Big Deals, lead by guitar player Tyler (aka "The Conductor"), drummer and producer Clinton (aka "The Wizard"), and keyboardist Nicholas (aka "Captain Keys"). The group is managed, meme'd, and morally supported by Kate (aka "The Reverend").
